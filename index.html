Thesis
Physical-world deeptech AI as the unlock

AI for the systems where deployment is the moat.

We focus on physical-world systems — robotics, embedded & edge intelligence, and autonomy — where the hard problems are data, evaluation, and the gap between simulation and reality. These are exactly the places where AI is the unlock, not the product.

We seed a handful of deep hypotheses each year, kill the ones that don't work fast, and give real time and capital to the ones that do.
